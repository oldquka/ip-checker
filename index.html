Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an IP address?
    An IP address is a unique identifier for your device on the internet. It helps websites and services route information to you.
  • Can my IP address reveal my location?
    Your IP address can give a rough approximation of your city or country, but never your precise physical address.
  • What is an ISP?
    ISP stands for Internet Service Provider. It's the company that provides you with internet access and assigns your public IP.
  • What is an ASN?
    ASN stands for Autonomous System Number, a unique code for networks that exchange traffic on the internet.
  • What are IPv4 and IPv6?
    IPv4 and IPv6 are different versions of IP addressing. IPv4 is the original format (e.g. 192.168.0.1), while IPv6 is newer and longer (e.g. 2001:db8::1).
  • How can I change my IP address?
    Usually, you can change your public IP by restarting your router or connecting through a VPN.
  • Is my data safe on this site?
    Yes. We do not store or share any of your IP or browsing data. All info is shown only to you, in your browser.
